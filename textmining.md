## Common Sources of Text
* Historical Newspapers
    - [Chronicling America](https://www.loc.gov/collections/chronicling-america/)

* Books out of copyright/in public domain
    - [Project Gutenberg](https://www.gutenberg.org/)
    -  [Internet Archive Books](https://archive.org/details/internetarchivebooks)
    - [Smithsonian Libraries](https://library.si.edu/books-online)

* Digitized archival material/primary sources
    - [eVols (UH Mānoa’s Digital Repository for Digitized Materials) - be sure to check for copyright](https://evols.library.manoa.hawaii.edu/home)
    - [Digital Public Library of America](https://dp.la/)
